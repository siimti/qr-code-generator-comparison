## Pricing

| Plan | Price | Notes |
|------|-------|-------|

| Growth | $250/mo, annual billing only ($3,000/yr; $6,000 over 24 months) | 150+ codes, API access, CRM integrations, live chat and priority email support, smart routing (conditional QR by day/device/location). A 10x price increase over Pro Plus with no intermediate option. |

Free plan: 2 dynamic codes, 500 scans (Flowcode branding on codes). Pro Plus: $25/mo billed annually ($300/yr) or $60/mo billed monthly (a 140% markup); 50 codes, 6,000 scans, no live support. Growth: $250/mo, annual-only ($3,000/yr; $6,000 over 24 months; $9,000 over 3 years) — a 10x jump from Pro Plus with no intermediate tier; required for API, CRM integrations, and live/priority support. Enterprise: custom pricing via AWS Marketplace contracts. Pro Plus 3-year cost is $900. No lifetime deal. 7-day free trial.

## Free Tier

Free plan with 2 dynamic codes and 500 scans (the Reddit roundup says roughly 500 and that it runs out fast). Flowcode branding appears on free- and lower-tier QR codes and is removable only by upgrading. The free trial is 7 days (vs Pageloot's 14, per the head-to-head comparison table). Refund policy is described as "Limited".

## Pros

- Artist-designed, premium-quality QR codes with custom dot patterns, brand kits, and high-resolution output — the head-to-head article says no platform produces more visually polished codes.
- Flowcode TV offers QR codes purpose-built for television, broadcast, sports, and streaming campaigns with fast scan rates.
- Enterprise-grade compliance and access: SOC2, GDPR, and CCPA certifications, SSO/SAML, API access, and custom contracts via AWS Marketplace.
- Advanced first-party demographic data collection that goes beyond standard scan tracking.
- A 4.6-star G2 rating (85 reviews) the articles call legitimate, with users praising design quality and customer support, and major clients including BMW, NBA, Kraft Heinz, and Delta.
- No ads injected on QR scans, plus extras like Flowtag digital business cards and built-in Flowpages landing pages.

## Cons

- A 10x pricing gap: API access, CRM integrations, and live/priority support require jumping from $25/mo Pro Plus straight to $250/mo Growth with no intermediate tier.
- Monthly billing penalty: Pro Plus costs $60/mo monthly vs $25/mo annual (140% markup), Growth is annual-only at $3,000/yr, and there is no lifetime deal or seasonal/project option.
- Pro Plus caps at 50 codes and 6,000 scans — limits the head-to-head article says agencies with 10+ clients hit within weeks, with busy campaigns exhausting the scan cap in days.
- Flowcode branding appears on QR codes from free and lower-tier plans and is removable only by upgrading.
- No agency-oriented features: no shareable client reports, no white-label dashboard below custom Enterprise, no built-in UTM tracking, no print templates, and no native Google Analytics integration (custom pixels are Enterprise-only).
- English-only landing pages and dashboard, organization by labels only (no folders/tags/notes), city-level-only scan tracking, limited upload file types, a 10% first-year-only affiliate program, and a limited refund policy.

## Key Features

- Artist-designed premium QR codes (custom dot patterns, brand kits, high-resolution output)

- Flowpages built-in landing pages
- Flowtag digital business cards
- Advanced first-party demographic data collection beyond standard scan analytics
- Smart routing / conditional QR by day, device, and location (Growth plan)
- API access and CRM integrations (Growth and up)
- SOC2, GDPR, and CCPA compliance; SSO/SAML on Enterprise
- White-label QR codes from Pro Plus up (white-label dashboard only on Enterprise)
- 3 multi-user seats on Pro Plus
- Available on AWS Marketplace; no ads injected on scans

## Watch-outs

- Only a 7-day free trial.
- Free-tier codes carry Flowcode branding removable only by upgrading.
- Monthly Pro Plus carries a 140% premium over annual, Growth is annual-only at $3,000/year, and Pro Plus caps (50 codes / 6,000 scans) push upgrades straight to $250/mo.
- A G2 reviewer wanting codes for just 4 concerts per year was reportedly forced into a full annual subscription.
- Refund policy is described as limited.
- Not HIPAA or ISO 27001 certified.
- Pageloot's own comparison tables rate Flowcode's trial-trap risk as Low and confirm it serves no ads on scans.
